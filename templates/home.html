Who makes Tomatoes Together?

Hi, I'm David Colgan. I created Tomatoes Together to address notification overload in my freelance work.

Is this a todo list app?

No. Tomatoes Together was intentionally designed to complement your todo list, not to replace it. Task management apps are a solved problem and you are probably already using one you like anyway.

Tomatoes Together is more of a done list - enter your current task and then it remembers everything you've worked on. Keep on using Trello, Asana, Todoist, or your plain paper lists!
